How it all began:


My passion for graphic design began in my senior year of high school, working on the yearbook. I used Adobe Photoshop to create cutouts of students involved in sports, and that experience sparked my interest in design. It wasn't until 2020, during the pandemic, that I realized how much I enjoyed editing and decided to pursue it as a career.
